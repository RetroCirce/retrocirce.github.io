Singing Voice Synthesis via Deep Learning Architectures

Jun Wang, Ke Chen

In this demo page, we present five pieces of singing voice synthesized from our deep learning model, for 203B course.
The left column shows the orginal singing voice sung by a professional female singer, in Children's Song Dataset.
The middle column shows the inverse of the mel-spectrogram, via the Hifi-GAN model, as the groundtruth of our training target.
The right column shows our synthesis result, compared with the middle column samples, we did a good synthesis by minimizing the mel-spectrogram L1-Norm loss, details are shown in the report.
However, the inverse of the mel-spectrogram by Hifi-GAN does not perform well as we can see there are many alising and pizzing sounds even between the left sapmles and middle samples.
Therefore, one of the future work is to tune the Hifi-GAN to a more accuracte stage and by doing this, we can have better synthesis quality.
