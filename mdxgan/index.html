MDX-GAN: Enhancing Perceptual Quality in Multi-Class Source Separation via Adversarial Training

In this appendix, we present the separation demos and additional experiment results of MDX-GAN.
(1) We present the separation demos of MDX-GAN and compare it with previous baselines over ten-class sound events.
(2) We provide additional experiment results, namely the per-class SDR and SI-SDR results on the validation and evaluation sets.
